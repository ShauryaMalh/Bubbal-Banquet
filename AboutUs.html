About Us

We at Saffron Banquet have mastered the art of creating beautiful memories. Luxurious ambience, contemporary styling, easy transportation links, elegant architecture and excellent service, this hall has it all.
Formerly known as CITY STAY, a trusted wedding facility, Saffron Banquet is taking the decade legacy forward with unique and matchless hospitality offerings.Located in the heart of the city at SECTOR-22, well connected with metro. Saffron Banquet is an elite, classic setting for your special event.
At Saffron Banquet, trust comes first. The services have been thoughtfully designed and developed taking into consideration the highest standards of comfort and convenience. We serve with utmost perfection, quality and care. Saffron firmly believes in the value of integrity and hard work and delivers the best hospitality experience customized for you.
Our Mission & Vision
At our banquet hall, our mission is to create unforgettable moments that celebrate life's milestones. With every event we host, whether it's a wedding, corporate gathering, or social affair, our goal is to provide a seamless and exceptional experience for our guests. We strive to exceed expectations by offering exquisite venues, personalized service, and attention to detail. Our vision is to be the premier destination for all special occasions, where dreams become reality. We aim to be known for our elegant ambiance, delectable cuisine, and impeccable service. Together, our mission and vision drive us to continuously elevate the art of celebration.
